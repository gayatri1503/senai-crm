# Pricing Policy

## Pricing Tiers
- **Starter**: $49/month — up to 3 users, basic features, 100 API calls/day
- **Standard**: $149/month — up to 15 users, full features, 1,000 API calls/day
- **Pro**: $299/month — up to 50 users, priority support, 10,000 API calls/day
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing — unlimited users, dedicated support, custom API limits

## Non-Profit Discount
Registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organisations qualify for a 30% discount on the Standard plan.
- Requires proof of non-profit status (EIN or registration document)
- Discount applies to base subscription only, not add-ons
- Non-profits are not eligible for Enterprise custom pricing without separate negotiation

## Pro-Rata Billing
- Mid-cycle upgrades are billed pro-rata for the remaining days in the billing period
- Example: Upgrading on day 15 of a 30-day cycle = 50% of the price difference charged immediately
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle — no pro-rata refunds for downgrades

## Enterprise Custom Pricing
- Contracts above 100 seats require a custom quote from the sales team
- Multi-year contracts receive additional discounts (10% for 2-year, 20% for 3-year)
- Enterprise accounts include a dedicated customer success manager

## Academic and Educational Pricing
- Universities and accredited educational institutions qualify for 40% off Standard plan
- Requires proof of institutional affiliation
- Available for up to 500 student seats per semester
