# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

## Uptime Guarantee
- Guaranteed uptime: 99.9% monthly (approximately 43.8 minutes of allowed downtime/month)
- Uptime is measured excluding scheduled maintenance windows
- Real-time status available at status.ourplatform.com

## Incident Response Times
- **P0 (Critical)**: System down or data loss — response within 15 minutes, all-hands response
- **P1 (High)**: Major feature unavailable — response within 1 hour
- **P2 (Medium)**: Degraded performance — response within 4 hours
- **P3 (Low)**: Minor issues — response within 1 business day

## SLA Credit Calculation
- 99.0% to 99.9% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 95.0% to 99.0% uptime: 25% credit of monthly fee
- Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly fee
- Credits are applied to the next invoice — no cash refunds for SLA breaches
- Credit requests must be submitted within 30 days of the incident

## Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
- P0 incidents: RCA delivered within 24 hours of resolution
- RCA must include: timeline, root cause, impact assessment, corrective actions
- Failure to deliver RCA within 24 hours entitles customer to additional 10% credit

## Scheduled Maintenance
- Maintenance windows: Saturdays 2–4 AM EST
- 72 hours advance notice required for planned maintenance
- Emergency maintenance may occur with shorter notice
