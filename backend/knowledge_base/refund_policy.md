# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Rules
- Refunds are available within 14 days of initial purchase only
- No refunds after the 14-day window under any circumstances
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days

## Exception Process
- Exceptions to the 14-day rule require approval from a senior support manager
- Valid exception grounds: billing error, duplicate charge, service never delivered
- Exception requests must be submitted via support ticket with documentation

## Credits vs Refunds
- For billing disputes outside the refund window, account credits are offered instead
- Credits never expire and apply to any future invoice
- Credits cannot be converted to cash

## Churn Retention Playbook
- Before processing any cancellation, offer a 20% discount for 3 months (retention offer)
- For VIP accounts (>$500/month), escalate to customer success manager before cancellation
- For accounts threatening public reviews: acknowledge urgency, offer direct call with support lead
- Three or more unanswered emails from a customer = immediate escalation to support lead
- Document all retention attempts in the CRM before marking account as churned

## Subscription Cancellation
- Cancellations take effect at end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for unused days after cancellation
- Data export available for 30 days after cancellation
