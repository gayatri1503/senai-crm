# Escalation Matrix

## Legal Threats
- Owner: Legal Team (legal@ourcompany.com)
- Response time: Acknowledge within 1 hour, legal review within 24 hours
- Do NOT auto-reply to cease and desist or lawsuit threats
- Includes: trademark disputes, data breach legal threats, contract disputes

## Security Incidents
- Owner: Security Team (security@ourcompany.com)
- Response time: Immediate (P0) — 15 minutes
- Includes: ransomware, data exfiltration threats, suspicious logins, credential compromise
- Never communicate with threat actors — escalate only

## GDPR and Compliance Requests
- Owner: Compliance Team (compliance@ourcompany.com)
- Response time: Acknowledge within 24 hours, fulfil within 30 days
- Auto-acknowledgement required citing 30-day statutory window
- Create internal compliance ticket for every GDPR request

## PR and Reputation Crisis
- Owner: Marketing + CEO (pr@ourcompany.com)
- Trigger: Customer threatens public review on G2, Trustpilot, Twitter, or press
- Response: Immediate escalation + retention offer from refund policy
- Check current public sentiment scores before responding

## VIP Churn Risk
- Owner: Customer Success (cs@ourcompany.com)
- Trigger: VIP account (>$500/month) sends cancellation or churn signal
- Response: CS manager personal outreach within 2 hours

## P0 Outage Escalation
- Owner: Engineering On-Call + CTO
- Trigger: Production system down, data loss, or SLA breach
- Response: All-hands within 15 minutes, status page update within 30 minutes
- RCA required within 24 hours of resolution

## Investor and Press Inquiries
- Owner: CEO + PR team
- Response: Acknowledge within 4 hours, schedule call within 24 hours
- Do not provide financial data or forward-looking statements without legal review
